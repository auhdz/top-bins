  - **Large Industrial Crate:** Extra capacity for bulky items
- **Weekly rates:**
  - Standard Bin: **$6.00** per week
  - Large Crate: **$9.00** per week
- **Refundable deposit:**
  - **$15** per Standard Bin
  - **$25** per Large Crate
- **Minimum rental:** 1 week (billed in weekly increments)
- **Full bundle:** choose **5 standard bins** or **5 large crates** for **2 weeks**; total due is quoted at checkout (weekly rate × five bins × two weeks, plus refundable deposits per bin); includes free delivery & pickup in Koreatown

- **Delivery window:** 24–48 hours after booking

You must return all bins in clean, good condition (normal wear and tear from normal use is acceptable).

Bins must be:

- Completely empty
- Free of all items, debris, dirt, hair, liquid, spills, or odors
- No stickers, tape, adhesive residue, or labels of any kind (this is a hard requirement)
- Not cracked, broken, or structurally damaged

You are responsible for any:

**Replacement costs** (charged if a bin is not returned or is damaged beyond repair):

- Standard 27-Gal Bin: **$35** each
- Large Crate: **$50**

If a bin is damaged but repairable, you will be charged the actual repair cost (minimum **$10** per bin).

## 5. Cleaning Fee

If bins are returned dirty, with residue, stickers, spills, or requiring extra cleaning:

- Cleaning fee: **$12** per bin

We will inspect every bin upon return. Photos are taken at delivery and pickup for your protection.

- **Late fee:** $2 per bin per day after the agreed pickup date.

## 7. Cancellation Policy
